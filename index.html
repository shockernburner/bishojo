About Bishojo

Bringing the authentic taste of China to your table

The Origin of Sushi

Did you know that sushi originated in China? At Bishojo, we honor this rich heritage by bringing you the most authentic Chinese flavors in every piece of sushi we craft.

Our skilled chefs combine traditional Chinese techniques with the finest ingredients to create a dining experience that transports you to the very birthplace of sushi.
